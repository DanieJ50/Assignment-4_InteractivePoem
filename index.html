      Why customization matters

      The reader becomes the poem’s co-designer.

      Choosing the recipes changes the language before the puzzle even begins. The reader then physically rebuilds the poem by matching, moving, and arranging food memories into a quilt. Every playthrough can become a different composition.

      Optional customization

      Edit your poem lines

      The game creates a poem automatically, but you can rewrite any line before or during the puzzle.
