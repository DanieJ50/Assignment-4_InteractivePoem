BerryBelle's Cozy Recipe Quilt

Match twelve CCD recipe tiles to their riddle-shaped quilt spaces. Every correct match stitches another line into the interactive poem.

How to play

Read a quilt riddle, then drag its matching recipe into the space.

You can also tap or click a recipe tile, then tap or click its matching quilt space. Keyboard players can use Enter or Space.

A Recipe Can Become a Place

The poem starts hidden. Each correct puzzle piece reveals its matching line in the original order.

    Why this interaction matters

    The reader physically rebuilds the poem.

    Recipes begin as scattered memories. Dragging, matching, and arranging them turns comfort food into language. The final quilt represents how Cozy Core combines creativity, comparison, and familiar food without treating joy as something that needs to disappear.
